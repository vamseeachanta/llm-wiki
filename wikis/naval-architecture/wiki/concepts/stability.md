# Ship Stability

Hydrostatic stability of floating vessels — the ability to return to upright equilibrium after displacement. This page covers the GZ-curve mechanics; criteria detail has been split into two sibling pages.

## Key Concepts

- **Metacentric height (GM)** — vertical distance between center of gravity and metacenter.
- **Righting arm (GZ)** — horizontal lever arm of restoring moment.
- **GZ curves** — righting arm vs heel angle; primary stability assessment tool.
- **Free surface effect** — reduction in effective GM from partially filled tanks.

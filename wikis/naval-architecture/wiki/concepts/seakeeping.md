# Seakeeping and Ship Motions

Vessel behavior in waves — motions, accelerations, and operability.

## Six Degrees of Freedom

- **Surge** — longitudinal motion (1st mode)
- **Sway** — transverse motion (2nd mode)
- **Heave** — vertical motion (3rd mode)
- **Roll** — rotation about longitudinal axis
- **Pitch** — rotation about transverse axis
- **Yaw** — rotation about vertical axis

## Key Topics

- **Seakeeping criteria** — motion limits for crew comfort, equipment operation, cargo safety
- **Response amplitude operators (RAOs)** — frequency-domain motion response
- **Wave spectra** — Pierson-Moskowitz, JONSWAP, ITT spectrum models
- **Added resistance in waves** — power increase in seaway
- **Seakeeping optimization** — hull form design for improved motion response
- **Operability assessment** — percentage of time operations can be performed

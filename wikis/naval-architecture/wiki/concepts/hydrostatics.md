# Ship Hydrostatics

Geometric and hydrostatic properties of ship hulls at rest.

## Key Parameters

- **Displacement (Δ)** — weight of water displaced by the hull
- **Block coefficient (Cb)** — hull fullness ratio
- **Waterplane area coefficient (Cwp)** — waterplane fullness
- **Midship section coefficient (Cm)** — midship section fullness
- **Prismatic coefficient (Cp)** — longitudinal distribution of volume
- **Tonnes per centimetre immersion (TPC)** — added weight for 1cm sinkage

## Curves and Plans

- **Bonjean curves** — cross-sectional area vs draught for loading calculations
- **Cross curves of stability** — righting arm vs displacement for all heel angles
- **Hydrostatic curves** — displacement, TPC, KM, MCTC vs draught
- **General arrangement plans** — vessel layout and compartmentation

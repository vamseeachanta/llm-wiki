# Ship Structural Design

Structural integrity of marine vessels under operational loads.

## Design Loads

- **Still water bending moment** — hull girder loading from weight distribution
- **Wave-induced bending moment** — dynamic loading from sea states
- **Shear force** — vertical and horizontal shear in hull girder
- **Torsion** — twisting of non-symmetric hull forms
- **Hydrostatic pressure** — hull side and bottom pressure
- **Dynamic loads** — slamming, green water, whipping, springing

## Structural Elements

- **Longitudinal framing** — stiffeners and girders in direction of hull axis
- **Transverse framing** — frames, floors, and beams
- **Mixed framing** — combination approaches
- **Hull girder strength** — primary structural capacity
- **Plate buckling** — local stability of stiffened panels
- **Fatigue** — cumulative damage under cyclic loading

## Classification Rules

- **ABS Rules** — American Bureau of Shipping classification
- **DNV Rules** — Det Norske Veritas structural requirements
- **LR Rules** — Lloyd's Register structural standards
- **IACS Common Structural Rules (CSR)** — unified bulk carrier and tanker rules

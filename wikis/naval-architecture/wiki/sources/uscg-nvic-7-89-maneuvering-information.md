# USCG NVIC 7-89 Maneuvering Information

Raw path: `/mnt/ace/acma-codes/USCG/NVIC's/1990 NVIC 7-89 Maneuvering Information.pdf`

## Relevance

Operational-information reference, not a mathematical model source.

## Extracted content

The OCR-derived review indicates that NVIC 7-89 standardizes maneuvering information display through:

- Pilot Card
- Wheelhouse Poster
- Maneuvering Booklet

The wheelhouse poster should include stopping distances and turning trajectories at maximum rudder angle in loaded/ballasted conditions. Performance shown may differ with environmental, hull, and loading conditions.

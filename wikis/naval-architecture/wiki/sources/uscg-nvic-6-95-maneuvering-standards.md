# USCG NVIC 6-95 Maneuvering Standards

Raw path: `/mnt/ace/acma-codes/USCG/NVIC's/1995 NVIC 6-95 Maneuvering Standards.pdf`

## Relevance

Local USCG source reproducing IMO maneuvering standards and explanatory guidance. Useful for validation metrics and operational standard context.

## Extracted criteria

- advance `<= 4.5 L`
- tactical diameter `<= 5 L`
- 10° rudder initial turning: travel `<= 2.5 L` before 10° heading change
- 10°/10° first overshoot criterion based on `L/V`
- 10°/10° second overshoot `<= first criterion + 15°`
- 20°/20° first overshoot `<= 25°`
- full-astern stopping track reach `<= 15 L`, with modifications possible for very large vessels

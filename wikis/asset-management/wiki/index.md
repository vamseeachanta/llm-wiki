# Knowledge Index: asset-management

*Updated 2026-05-03 by W1-B topical scaffold (issue #2587). Sanctioned-by #2615.*

## Scope boundary

This wiki covers **engineering / integrity asset management** (asset register,
life-extension, FFS, ALARP, RAM, RBI, performance standards under safety-case
regimes, ISO 55000 family). It does **NOT** cover **financial / portfolio**
asset management (see `assethold` for that domain — the only `wiki/sources/`
entry on this wiki at present is a property-portfolio corpus that is
out-of-scope for engineering asset management as defined here).

In short: engineering / integrity asset management is in scope; financial /
portfolio asset management is NOT in scope.

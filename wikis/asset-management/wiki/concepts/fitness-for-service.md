# Fitness-for-Service (FFS)

## Scope

Engineering / integrity asset management — see scope-boundary block in [index.md](../index.md). Engineering evaluation of in-service equipment containing damage / flaws / out-of-design conditions.

## Definition

Fitness-for-Service is a quantitative engineering assessment that determines whether an item containing a flaw or damage is adequate for continued operation under defined conditions, and if so, on what re-rating, re-inspection, repair, or monitoring basis.

## Methodology / Mechanics

- Tiered approach: Level 1 (screening), Level 2 (analytical), Level 3 (numerical / FEA).
- Damage-mechanism-specific (general metal loss, local thin areas, pitting, crack-like flaws, creep, fire damage, etc.).
- Drives run / repair / replace / re-rate decisions.

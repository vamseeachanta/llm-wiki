# IAM Competency Model

## Scope

Engineering / integrity asset management — see scope-boundary block in [index.md](../index.md). Competency curriculum for asset-management practitioners; intentionally NOT scaffolded as a `wiki/standards/` page because IAM Competency Framework is a curriculum, not a code-with-revision under the citation contract.

## Definition

The Institute of Asset Management (IAM) Competency Framework v3.0 specifies 27 competencies grouped under 7 subject areas, each defined at multiple proficiency levels. It is the practitioner-side curriculum complement to ISO 55001's organizational requirements.

## Methodology / Mechanics

- Competency assessment is gap-driven: current vs. required level by role.
- Used by employers for capability planning and by individuals for CPD targeting.
- Reaffirmed by IAM following its original publication; remains the canonical curriculum.

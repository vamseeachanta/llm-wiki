# Life Extension

## Scope

Engineering / integrity asset management — see scope-boundary block in [index.md](../index.md). Late-life operating practice: extending service life of an installation beyond the original design life through engineering re-evaluation.

## Definition

Life extension is the structured engineering and integrity-management process by which an asset is justified for continued service past its original design life, typically driven by economic value of remaining hydrocarbons or service requirement.

## Methodology / Mechanics

- Re-evaluate design basis vs. current load / damage history.
- FFS assessment of degraded components.
- Updated RBI plan with shorter intervals or upgraded techniques.
- Updated Performance Standards reflecting reduced design margins.

# Safety-Critical Element (SCE) Classification

## Scope

Engineering / integrity asset management — see scope-boundary block in [index.md](../index.md). Classification of safety-critical equipment for major-accident-hazard control.

## Definition

A Safety-Critical Element is any part of an installation (equipment, structure, system) whose failure could cause or contribute substantially to a major accident, OR whose purpose is to prevent or limit the effect of a major accident.

## Methodology / Mechanics

- Bowtie analysis is the dominant identification method (barriers on both prevention and mitigation legs).
- Each SCE is bound to a written Performance Standard.
- Consequence classification (NORSOK Z-008) supplies the methodology in NCS practice.

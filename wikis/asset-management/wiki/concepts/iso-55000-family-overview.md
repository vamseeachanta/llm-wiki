# ISO 55000 Family Overview

## Scope

Engineering / integrity asset management — see scope-boundary block in [index.md](../index.md). High-level orientation page for the ISO 55000 family. Defers the asset-lifecycle definition to the canonical [Asset Lifecycle](./asset-lifecycle.md) page.

## Definition

The ISO 55000 family is a three-part international standard covering vocabulary (55000), management-system requirements (55001), and application guidance (55002). It is the organizing umbrella for any organization claiming a recognized asset-management system.

## Methodology / Mechanics

- 55000 = terminology and principles (the dictionary).
- 55001 = the auditable requirements (the checklist).
- 55002 = practical guidance on satisfying 55001 (the cookbook).
- The 2014 edition replaced PAS 55-1/-2:2008 (BSI) for new certifications.

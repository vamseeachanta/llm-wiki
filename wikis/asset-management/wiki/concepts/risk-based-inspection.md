# Risk-Based Inspection (RBI)

## Scope

Engineering / integrity asset management — see scope-boundary block in [index.md](../index.md). RBI is the canonical inspection-prioritization methodology used across upstream, downstream, and offshore practice.

## Definition

Risk-Based Inspection is a structured decision-making process that uses probability and consequence of failure to prioritize inspection scope, technique, frequency, and depth across a population of fixed equipment.

## Methodology / Mechanics

- Damage-mechanism identification per equipment type / process service.
- PoF estimation (generic + condition-modifier).
- CoF estimation (safety / environment / production).
- Risk = PoF × CoF; mapped to inspection interval / coverage / technique.

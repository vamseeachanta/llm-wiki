# Performance Standards

## Scope

Engineering / integrity asset management — see scope-boundary block in [index.md](../index.md). Engineering performance standards under safety-case regimes; NOT HR or financial performance management.

## Definition

A Performance Standard (PS) is a written statement, expressed in measurable / verifiable terms, of the performance required of a Safety-Critical Element (SCE) in the context of a major-accident hazard. Each PS describes functionality, availability, reliability, survivability, and interactions with other SCEs (FARSI).

## Methodology / Mechanics

- Each SCE → ≥ 1 PS.
- PS attributes verified by inspection, testing, or analysis on a defined assurance frequency.
- PS gaps are reported through the safety-case verification scheme.

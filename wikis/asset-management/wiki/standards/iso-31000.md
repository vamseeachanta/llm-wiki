# ISO 31000:2018 — Risk management — Guidelines

## Scope

ISO 31000 provides principles, framework, and process for managing risk. It is invoked by ISO 55001 §6 (risk-based actions on the asset management system) and by every downstream RBI / SCE / ALARP methodology that requires a defensible risk-management foundation.

## Where it applies

Any engineering integrity program that needs a risk-management substrate beneath RBI methodologies (API RP 580/581, DNV-RP-G101), beneath safety-case regimes (HSE SCR15), or beneath ISO 55001 conformity claims.

# ISO 55002:2018 — Asset management — Application guidance

## Scope

ISO 55002 provides interpretive guidance on applying ISO 55001 requirements. The 2018 revision expanded the guidance to cover more practical implementation patterns, including the strategic asset management plan (SAMP), data and information requirements, and asset-management decision-making.

## Where it applies

Implementation teams operationalizing ISO 55001 within an organization. Used as the practitioner-facing reference for moving from "we have a policy" to "we have a working management system".

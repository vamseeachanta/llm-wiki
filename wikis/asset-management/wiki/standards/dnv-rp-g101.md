# DNV-RP-G101 — Risk-Based Inspection of Offshore Topsides Static Mechanical Equipment

## Scope

DNV-RP-G101 provides a recommended practice for risk-based inspection (RBI) of static mechanical equipment on offshore topsides — vessels, columns, heat exchangers, piping, etc. It blends qualitative screening with semi-quantitative damage-mechanism inputs and is widely used as the offshore-sector companion to API RP 580/581.

## Where it applies

Offshore production platforms, FPSOs, and FLNGs where the operator has either regulatory or owner mandate to use a DNV recommended-practice basis for inspection planning, often in combination with sector safety-case requirements.

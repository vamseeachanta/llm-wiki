# ISO 55000:2014 — Asset management — Overview, principles and terminology

## Scope

ISO 55000 provides the overview, principles and terminology that frame the ISO 55000 family. It is the vocabulary backbone for ISO 55001 (requirements) and ISO 55002 (application guidance) and defines the common lexicon used across organizational asset management systems.

## Where it applies

Engineering and integrity asset management programs that need a common vocabulary for `asset`, `asset management`, `asset management system`, `asset portfolio`, `value`, and the line of sight from organizational objectives to asset-level decisions. Cited by tendering, audit, certification, and regulatory regimes worldwide.

# ISO 55001:2014 — Asset management — Management systems — Requirements

## Scope

ISO 55001 specifies requirements for an asset management system within the context of the organization. It is the auditable / certifiable standard in the family — the basis on which third-party certification bodies issue ISO 55001 conformity certificates.

## Where it applies

Organizations seeking to demonstrate (to regulators, owners, lenders, or insurers) that their asset management system is structured and operating to a recognized international standard. Section 6 explicitly invokes risk management aligned to ISO 31000.

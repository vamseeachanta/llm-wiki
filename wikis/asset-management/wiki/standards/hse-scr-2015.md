# HSE Offshore Installations (Offshore Safety Case) Regulations 2015 (UK SCR15)

## Scope

SCR15 is the UK statutory instrument governing offshore safety cases. It places duty on duty-holders to identify Safety-Critical Elements (SCEs), set Performance Standards (PS) for them, and verify those PS are met for the life of the installation.

## Where it applies

UK Continental Shelf (UKCS) offshore installations and any export contract referencing UK safety-case duty. The PS / SCE vocabulary it formalizes is widely adopted beyond UKCS as a de-facto template.

# NORSOK Z-008 — Risk-based maintenance and consequence classification

## Scope

NORSOK Z-008 establishes principles for risk-based maintenance and the consequence classification of equipment on Norwegian Continental Shelf (NCS) installations. It defines the methodology for ranking equipment by safety, environment, and production-loss consequence and links the classification to maintenance-program intensity.

## Where it applies

NCS-regulated installations where Petroleum Safety Authority (PSA) expectations align to the NORSOK substrate. Increasingly referenced in non-Norwegian operator integrity manuals as a clear consequence-classification template.

## NEW: Depth-pilot saturation criterion (V14)

**Definition.** The corpus declares **depth-pilot saturation** when ALL of the following hold for the most recent depth-pilot iter:

1. **Median per-iter line-growth-per-page < 50%** AND median per-iter word-growth-per-page < 75%.
2. **Zero pages remaining at thin-starter cohort** (≤50L OR ≤200w) across all three wikis.
3. **Zero pages remaining at mid-tier cohort with inbound-files ≥20**.
4. **Cross-wiki bridge density unchanged for 3+ consecutive audits** (V14 already at 6).

**Rationale.** The 4-element pattern's leverage decays exponentially with starting-page-thickness. Once thin-starter and high-traffic mid-tier candidates are exhausted, the per-iter ROI on depth work drops below the per-iter ROI of pivoting to a different quality axis (link verification, frontmatter validation, citation coverage).

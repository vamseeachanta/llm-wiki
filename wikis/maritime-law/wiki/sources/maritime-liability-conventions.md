# 6 International Maritime Liability Conventions

Seed migration from `knowledge/seeds/maritime-liabilities.yaml`.

## Summary Table

| Convention | Year | Scope | Key Feature |
|-----------|------|-------|-------------|
| CLC | 1992 | Oil tanker spills | Strict liability, compulsory insurance, $140M cap |
| Bunker Convention | 2001 | Bunker fuel spills | Non-tanker oil pollution liability |
| HNS Convention | 2010 | Hazardous substances | Noxious substances + supplemental fund |
| Athens Convention | 2002 | Passengers | Death/injury to passengers at sea |
| LLMC | 1976/1996 | General claims | General limitation of all maritime claims |
| OPA 90 | 1990 | US waters only | Unlimited gross-negligence liability |

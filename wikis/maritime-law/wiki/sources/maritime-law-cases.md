## Pattern: Evolution of Liability

1. **1967-1978**: Strict liability, compensation funds established (CLC)
2. **1996-2002**: Corporate criminal liability, port authority responsibility
3. **2010+**: Unlimited gross-negligence liability (OPA 90), multi-defendant apportionment

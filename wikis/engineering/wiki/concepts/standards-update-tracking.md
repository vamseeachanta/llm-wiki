# Standards Update Tracking (2025-2026)

Key findings from nightly research outputs tracking offshore engineering standards changes relevant to the digitalmodel codebase.

## Active Updates

### DNV-RP-C203 (2024 Edition)
Revised fatigue analysis methodology: new S-N curves for seawater with cathodic protection, updated notch model, increased fatigue strength from reassessed test data. **Impact**: spectral fatigue module needs curve updates from 2016-era baseline.

### DNV July 2025 Edition (Effective 1 Jan 2026)
119 documents published. Restructured structural standards for column-stabilized units (OS-C103), TLPs (OS-C105), and deep-draught floating units (OS-C106). 2026 edition hearing closed 12 April 2026. **Impact**: YAML traceability manifests need updated identifiers.

### ABS Offshore Rules Consolidation (Effective 1 Jan 2026)
Legacy FPI Rules and Facilities Rules withdrawn. All new offshore unit design must use consolidated Offshore Rules and Facilities Guide. **Impact**: website marketing copy, not calculation code.

### API 579-1/ASME FFS-1 Part 16 (In Development)
New Part 16 adds assessment methods for FRP (Fiber Reinforced Polymer) equipment. 4th Edition (Dec 2021) remains current for steel. **Impact**: future fitness-for-service module expansion.

### ISO 19901-4:2025 (Geotechnical)
Third edition superseding 2016. Adds unified CPT-based pile design for sands, intermediate foundation guidance, pile capacity reassessment clause. **Impact**: future on-bottom stability module inputs.

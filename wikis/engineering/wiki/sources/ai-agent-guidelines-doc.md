## Summary

Critical instructions for ALL AI agents defining the cross-review policy, mandatory approval workflows, and agent coordination rules. Covers Claude, Codex, Gemini, and any other AI agents.

## Key Extractions

- **Cross-review policy**: Claude orchestrates, Codex reviews adversarially, Gemini optional third
- **Two-provider review by default**, three-provider only when justified
- **Ask before proceeding** mandate for all significant decisions
- Review verdicts normalized to: APPROVE, MINOR, MAJOR

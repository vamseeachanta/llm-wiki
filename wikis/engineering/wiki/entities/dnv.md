# DNV

DNV is a classification society and assurance organization whose rules, standards, recommended practices, and verification services are widely used in offshore, maritime, pipeline, floating-production, and marine-operations engineering.

In this wiki, DNV is treated as a publisher and classification-society entity rather than as a source of copied standards text. The public repository stores bounded resolver pages and authored summaries that help engineers route calculations and design-basis references to the right public metadata target.

## Engineering Relevance

DNV material appears across several engineering workstreams:

- offshore structural fatigue and environmental loading
- submarine pipelines, free spans, on-bottom stability, and corrosion assessment
- position mooring and station-keeping design basis
- dynamic riser and marine-operations analysis
- cathodic protection and asset-integrity workflows

## Public-Wiki Boundaries

This page does not reproduce DNV rule clauses, tables, formulas, acceptance criteria, or vendor PDF text. It is a navigation and entity page that links to metadata-only resolver pages already committed in the public wiki.

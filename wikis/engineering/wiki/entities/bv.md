# Bureau Veritas

Bureau Veritas (BV) is a classification society and assurance organization associated with marine and offshore classification, survey, certification, and rule publication.

In this wiki, BV is represented as a publisher/classification-society entity. Existing public metadata indicates a large BV ship-and-offshore rules source family, but publisher-specific standards pages remain deferred until public revision metadata is independently verified.

## Engineering Relevance

BV is relevant to:

- ship and offshore classification contexts
- comparison of classification-society rule families
- future metadata-only standards routing once revision, title, and public-catalog evidence are verified
- marine assurance and survey workflows adjacent to DNV, ABS, and LR coverage

## Public-Wiki Boundaries

This page does not reproduce BV rule clauses, tables, formulas, acceptance criteria, or vendor PDF text. It records only a public-safe entity anchor and points to committed metadata pages that already note the deferred BV standards-promotion boundary.

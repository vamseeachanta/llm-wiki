# American Bureau of Shipping

The American Bureau of Shipping (ABS) is a classification society whose rules and guides are used for ships, offshore installations, FPSOs, risers, offshore structural fatigue, and cathodic-protection contexts.

In this wiki, ABS is represented as a publisher and classification-society entity. The committed standards pages are bounded metadata and resolver targets for public navigation and citation routing; they are not copies of ABS rule books.

## Engineering Relevance

ABS-linked wiki material supports:

- offshore-installation classification and conditions-of-classification routing
- steel-vessel hull and structural context
- FPSO design and dynamic-loading references
- offshore-riser guide discovery
- fatigue and cathodic-protection summary navigation

## Public-Wiki Boundaries

This page does not reproduce ABS clauses, tables, formulas, acceptance criteria, or vendor PDF text. It only connects committed public metadata and authored synthesis pages.

# Lloyd's Register

Lloyd's Register is a classification society and assurance organization associated with ship and offshore classification, rule development, survey, and technical assurance.

In this wiki, Lloyd's Register is included as a first-class entity so future standards or rule-page promotion can route through a stable publisher page. The current public repo has fewer LR-specific resolver pages than DNV or ABS, so this page is intentionally conservative.

## Engineering Relevance

LR is relevant to:

- ship and offshore classification decisions
- marine assurance and survey workflows
- comparison of classification-society rule coverage across DNV, ABS, LR, and BV
- future standards-routing work where LR publications are promoted into `wiki/standards/`

## Public-Wiki Boundaries

This page does not reproduce LR rule clauses, tables, formulas, acceptance criteria, or vendor PDF text. It is a public navigation anchor and publisher entity page.

# API RP 11S7 — ESP Seal Chamber Sections

## Scope

API RP 11S7 covers the seal section (sometimes called the "protector") that sits between the ESP motor and pump. The seal section's role is **load-bearing for ESP reliability** — it isolates the dielectric motor oil from wellbore fluid, equalizes downhole pressure across the motor housing, and absorbs axial thrust from the pump.

## Seal-section design dimensions

- **Bag-style** — flexible oil bag separates motor oil from well fluid; volumetric expansion absorbs thermal cycling
- **Labyrinth-style** — series of chambers with U-tube oil paths; relies on density difference between motor oil and well fluid
- **Hybrid** — combines bag + labyrinth elements for harder-service conditions (high-temperature, high-water-cut, high-solids)

## Failure modes addressed by RP 11S7

- Seal-oil ingress (well fluid into motor) — leads to electrical short
- Motor-oil egress (motor oil into well) — leads to environmental release + motor over-heating
- Thermal-cycling fatigue on bag elastomers
- Thrust-bearing overload

## Where it applies

ESP-component manufacturers qualifying seal-section designs; operator critical-service selection (HPHT, sour, high-solids).

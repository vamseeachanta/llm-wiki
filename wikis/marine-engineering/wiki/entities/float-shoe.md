# Float Shoe

A cementing tool installed at the bottom of the casing string. It acts as a guide for running
the casing into the wellbore and provides a check valve to prevent cement backflow during displacement.

## Key Features

- **Nose configuration**: Rounded (better cement flow) or wedge nose (steering capability)
- **Valve type**: Super Seal II — extra large flow area
- **Pressure rating**: 5000 psi static
- **Temperature rating**: 400 deg F
- **Drill-out**: Easy drill-out with aluminum construction

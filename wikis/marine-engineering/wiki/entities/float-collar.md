# Float Collar

A cementing tool installed 1–2 joints above the bottom of the casing string. It contains a
check valve that prevents backflow of cement during the displacement phase of primary cementing operations.

## Key Features

- **Valve type**: Super Seal II (Halliburton) — large flow area, reliable sealing
- **Drill-out**: Aluminum construction for easy drill-out with standard or PDC bits
- **Pressure rating**: Up to 5000 psi static conditions
- **Temperature rating**: Up to 400 deg F
- **Size range**: Typically 4-1/2" to 20"

## Well Construction Context

Placed 1–2 joints above the float shoe to create a "cement catch" zone, ensuring adequate
cement column height even if the bottom shoe fails.

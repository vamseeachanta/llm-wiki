# Tension-Leg Platform

A tension-leg platform (TLP) is a floating offshore platform restrained vertically by tensioned tendons connected to seabed foundations. The tendon system strongly limits heave, roll, and pitch while allowing horizontal offsets governed by tendon geometry and environmental loading.

## Engineering Role

TLP engineering typically focuses on:

- tendon pretension and dynamic response
- hull, tendon, foundation, and riser coupling
- setdown, offset, and air-gap behavior
- fatigue and extreme-load checks
- top-tensioned riser and dry-tree interfaces
- installation and tendon hook-up operations

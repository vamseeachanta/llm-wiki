# FLNG

An FLNG unit is a floating liquefied natural gas facility that produces, liquefies, stores, and exports LNG offshore or nearshore. It couples LNG process systems with floating-unit marine engineering, station-keeping, transfer operations, and metocean operability.

## Engineering Role

FLNG pages belong at the boundary between LNG-project knowledge and marine engineering:

- station-keeping and mooring design
- LNG carrier approach, berthing, or side-by-side transfer envelopes
- sloshing, motion, and process-topsides operability
- loading-arm or hose compatibility
- nearshore/offshore metocean sensitivity
- emergency disconnection and cargo-stop criteria

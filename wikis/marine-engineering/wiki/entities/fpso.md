# FPSO

An FPSO is a floating production, storage, and offloading unit. It combines offshore hydrocarbon processing, storage, export, marine systems, station-keeping, and operations in one floating asset.

## Engineering Role

FPSO engineering links several wiki topics:

- hull motions, seakeeping, and stability
- spread mooring, turret mooring, or other station-keeping systems
- riser and umbilical interfaces
- topsides processing and utilities
- offloading operations and tandem-mooring interfaces
- integrity, inspection, fatigue, corrosion, and marine operations

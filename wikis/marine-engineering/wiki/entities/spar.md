# Spar

A spar is a deep-draft floating offshore platform with a long vertical hull that provides buoyancy, stability, and motion characteristics suited to deepwater production and dry-tree riser configurations.

## Engineering Role

Spar engineering typically considers:

- deep draft and heave/roll/pitch response
- hard-tank, truss, or cell-style hull architecture
- top-tensioned riser and export-riser interfaces
- station-keeping and mooring fatigue
- vortex-induced motion sensitivity
- installation, tow-out, upending, and offshore hook-up operations

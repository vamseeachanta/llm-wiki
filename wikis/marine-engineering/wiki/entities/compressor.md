# Compressor (Gas)

Equipment used to increase the pressure of gas streams in oil and gas processing facilities.
Critical for gas export, gas lift, and vapor recovery operations.

## Types in Offshore Service

| Type | Application | Pressure Range |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Centrifugal | Gas export, large-volume streams | Low to moderate |
| Reciprocating | Gas injection, vapor recovery | High pressure |
| Screw | Fuel gas, utility service | Low to moderate |

## Key Design Requirements (per DNVGL-OS-E201)

- **Surge protection**: Anti-surge control systems required
- **Materials**: Sour service requirements per NACE MR0175 where H2S present
- **Seals**: Dry gas seals for process compressors
- **Auxiliary systems**: Lube oil, seal gas, cooling water

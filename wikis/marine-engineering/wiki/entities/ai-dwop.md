# AI in Drill Well on Paper (DWOP)

Drill Well on Paper (DWOP) is a pre-spud planning exercise where the well is drilled
on paper before mobilisation. Engineers walk through every section, identifying risks
and setting parameters. GenAI is being applied to automate and standardise this process.

## Traditional DWOP Process

- Pre-spud planning exercise — walk through every section on paper before drilling
- Traditionally experience-driven and manual
- Covers all well sections: surface, intermediate, production intervals
- Identifies risks, sets parameters, establishes contingency plans

## AI/GenAI Application (Akshay Manjramkar, Jindal Drilling)

- **Automated well program parsing**: parse existing well programs into structured data
- **Execution-ready plan generation**: generate standardised execution plans from parsed data
- **Standardised workflows**: consistent format across wells and rigs
- Claimed ~98% faster planning cycles

## Design Patterns

- DWOP is a pre-spud planning exercise — walk through every section on paper before drilling
- AI DWOP value proposition: parse existing well programs, generate execution plans, standardise workflows
- 98% faster claim should be treated sceptically — speed without domain competence is dangerous in drilling
- Domain experts consistently caution: AI augments, does not replace drilling judgment
- Data quality and sourcing is the bottleneck for AI in drilling, not the algorithm
- Planning-to-execution feedback loop is where real value compounds

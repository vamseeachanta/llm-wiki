# Jacket Platform

A jacket platform is a fixed offshore structure made from a braced tubular steel frame, typically piled to the seabed and supporting topsides, risers, conductors, and utilities.

## Engineering Role

Jacket engineering normally involves:

- tubular-joint and brace-member strength
- pile and foundation capacity
- wave, current, marine-growth, and fatigue loading
- launch, lifting, upending, and installation analysis
- corrosion protection and inspection planning
- topsides, riser, conductor, and boat-landing interfaces

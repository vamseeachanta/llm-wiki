# Separator (Production)

A pressure vessel used to separate produced hydrocarbon fluids into gas, oil, and water phases.
Fundamental equipment in offshore oil and gas processing systems.

## Types

| Type | Description | Location |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Production separator | Primary 2-phase or 3-phase separation | Topside process module |
| Test separator | Used for well production testing | Topside |
| Scrubber | Removes liquid droplets from gas stream | Gas compression |
| Slug catcher | Handles large liquid slugs from pipelines | Pipeline inlet |

## Design Considerations (per DNVGL-OS-E201)

- **Pressure class**: Determined by operating pressure + safety margin
- **Residence time**: Oil retention typically 2–10 min; gas residence governed by droplet settling
- **Level control**: High/low level alarms, shutdown settings
- **Sour service**: NACE MR0175 material requirements for H2S environments
- **Fire protection**: Fireproofing, deluge, pressure relief

# Mooring Failures at LNG Terminals

Compilation of mooring line failure incidents and investigations at LNG terminals worldwide, sourced from public investigation reports, conference papers, and industry databases.

## Key Incidents

| # | Incident | Date | Root Cause | Key Lesson |
|---|----------|------|------------|------------|
| 1 | NWS LNG multi-year failures | 2005-2014 | Long-period swell resonance | 50mm swell can part lines via resonance |
| 2 | LNG Zarga at South Hook | 2015 | HMPE line failure | Severe crew injury, MAIB investigation |
| 3 | Prelude FLNG GALLINA | 2018 | Nylon line failure | NOPSEMA Incident 5415 |
| 4 | 7 HMPE lines BW Fleet | Various | Material degradation | Industry-wide HMPE pattern 2007-2011 |
| 5 | Golar Freeze Elba Island | 2006 | Passing vessel wake | Federal Register security zone |
| 6 | Summit FSRU Bangladesh | 2021 | Mooring line tear | 3 months out of service |
| 7 | Ansac Splendor Longview | 2018 | Spring line snap-back | 2 fatalities |
| 8 | Veracruz Port | 2024-2025 | Mooring failures | 5 failures in 6 months |
| 9 | MV Asterix Busan | 2023 | HMPE snap-back | TSB Canada M23F0012 |
| 10 | Cyclone Narelle NWS | Mar 2026 | Extreme weather | Gorgon, Wheatstone, NWS LNG interrupted |

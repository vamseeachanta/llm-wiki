# RB122: Float Equipment (Halliburton)

## Summary

Halliburton's RB122 catalog for float equipment used in well cementing operations. Covers
float collars, float shoes, cementing plugs, and related accessories for cement placement
in oil and gas wells.

## Key Content

- **Super Seal II Float Collar** — compact design, suitable for PDC bit drilling
- **Float Shoe** — rounded or wedge nose configuration
- **Operating limits** — 400 deg F temperature, 5000 psi pressure (static conditions)
- **Cementing plugs** — SRP (Super Seal II) plug set for cement displacement
- **Aluminum composition** — minimum aluminum content for easy drill-out

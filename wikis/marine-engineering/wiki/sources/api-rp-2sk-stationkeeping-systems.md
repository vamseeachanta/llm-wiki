## Summary

American Petroleum Institute recommended practice for design and analysis of stationkeeping systems for floating offshore structures. Covers catenary, taut-leg, semi-taut mooring, and single-point mooring (CALM, SALM, turret) systems. Addresses environmental loading, mooring analysis methods (quasi-static, dynamic, coupled), safety factors, and inspection requirements. Key reference for offshore mooring engineering with 2008 addendum.

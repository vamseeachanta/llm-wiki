# Piping Component E-books for Oil & Gas Engineer

## Summary

Reference guide covering fundamental piping components used in process piping for oil and
gas engineering. Covers pipes, fittings, flanges, and gaskets with practical engineering
knowledge.

## Key Content

- **Pipes** — specifications, schedules, material grades
- **Pipe Fittings** — elbows, tees, reducers, caps, unions
- **Flanges** — welding neck, slip-on, blind, socket weld, threaded
- **Gaskets** — ring type, spiral wound, sheet gaskets for flange sealing

# DNV-RP-B401: Cathodic Protection Design

## Summary

DNV Recommended Practice RP-B401 provides guidance for the design of cathodic protection systems
for offshore structures. It covers both galvanic (sacrificial) and impressed current cathodic
protection systems for fixed steel, floating, and subsea structures.

## Key Content

- **Anode sizing methodology** — calculation of required anode mass, number, and dimensions
- **Current density requirements** — recommended values for different marine environment zones
- **Anode placement** — guidelines for anode distribution to ensure adequate potential distribution
- **Design parameters** — seawater resistivity, coating breakdown factors, design life
- **Zonal requirements** — different current densities for splash zone, submerged zone, mud zone

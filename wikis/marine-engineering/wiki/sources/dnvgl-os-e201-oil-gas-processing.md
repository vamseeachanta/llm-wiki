# DNVGL-OS-E201: Oil and Gas Processing Systems

## Summary

DNVGL-OS-E201 is an offshore standard covering oil and gas processing systems on fixed and
floating installations. It addresses design, fabrication, testing, and operational requirements
for topside process equipment including separators, compressors, pumps, heat exchangers, and
pressure safety systems.

## Key Content

- **Processing system classification** — categorization by safety criticality
- **Design requirements** — pressure vessels, piping, equipment layout
- **Materials selection** — appropriate materials for sour service, H2S, CO2 environments
- **Pressure safety** — relief valve sizing, flare systems
- **Testing and qualification** — FAT, SAT, commissioning requirements
- **Supersedes DNV-OS-E201 (December 2012)**

# Overview: Acma Projects

The `acma-projects` wiki captures project-specific engineering source intelligence before reusable workflows are implemented in analysis repos such as `digitalmodel`.

## Current focus

- **B1528 SIROCCO Breakaway** — source intelligence for rudder geometry, rudder-force/yaw-moment workbook logic, SIROCCO time-trace evidence, and future project-specific yaw-moment / turning-trace reports.

## Operating principles

- Treat ACMA project repositories as source-of-record; do not copy confidential/raw project files into the wiki.
- Store concise source summaries, extracted public-safe calculation parameters, and crosswalks to exact repo paths.
- Separate source evidence from preliminary modeling assumptions.
- For engineering calculations, pair project-specific evidence with reusable methods in `digitalmodel` and preserve caveats about standards/compliance scope.

# P&A Rigless Operations

## Scope

Rigless P&A means executing plug-and-abandonment operations **without mobilizing a drilling rig**. Uses well-services equipment (coiled tubing, wireline, snubbing, hydraulic workover units) to install barriers and remove surface infrastructure. Cost savings vs rig-mobilized P&A are substantial — 40-70% cost reduction in some scenarios — driving widespread adoption for mature-basin abandonment campaigns.

## When rigless P&A is feasible

- Single-zone wells with simple completion architecture
- Wells where existing tubing can stay (or removed via snubbing)
- Wells where required barrier depths are accessible via existing wellbore restrictions
- Wells with low pressure / non-sour / standard service

## When rigless P&A is NOT feasible

- Multi-zone wells with intricate barrier requirements at multiple depths
- Wells where casing must be cut + retrieved (typically requires rig)
- Wells with high pressure or sour service where rigless equipment is under-rated
- Wells requiring extensive downhole interventions (milling, sidetracking, etc.)

## Typical rigless P&A workflow

1. **Mobilize CT or wireline unit** to wellhead
2. **Set mechanical plug** at first barrier depth via wireline or CT-conveyed setting tool
3. **Test plug integrity** (positive + negative pressure tests)
4. **Set cement plug above mechanical plug** via CT-conveyed dump bailer or cement squeeze
5. **Test cement plug**
6. **Repeat for upper barriers** as required by jurisdictional regulations
7. **Remove surface infrastructure** — Christmas tree disassembled; wellhead cut + capped (offshore: below seabed)

## Cost-economics framework

| Cost element | Rig-mobilized | Rigless |
|---|---|---|
| Rig day rate | $200K-$1M/day | $0 |
| Service-unit day rate (CT/wireline) | $0 (in rig scope) | $20K-$80K/day |
| Personnel | rig crew + services | services crew only |
| Mobilization | rig-mobilization cost | service-unit mobilization (~10%) |
| Total typical P&A duration | 5-20 days | 3-10 days |
| Net cost | $1-10M+ | $0.5-5M |

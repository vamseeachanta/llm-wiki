## Scope boundary

This wiki covers **drilling engineering and well construction** — rig technical specifications, drilling equipment, drilling-fluid systems, well planning, AFE / RFP / tender evaluation methodology, offset-well analysis, and AI/ML practitioner literature applied to drilling. It does **NOT** cover production operations, reservoir engineering, or downstream. See [Overview](overview.md) for the full scope boundary.

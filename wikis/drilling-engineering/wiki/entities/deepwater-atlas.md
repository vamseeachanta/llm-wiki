# Deepwater Atlas

## Scope

Specific named rig entity for **Deepwater Atlas** — Transocean's ultra-deepwater drillship, Gen 7+ class, designed for 20K-psi HPHT applications. Among the most-capable drillships in the global fleet as of mid-2020s.

## Public spec summary

(Paraphrased from operator public fleet status; canonical source: Transocean's fleet status report at deepwater.com/our-fleet/our-rigs)

- **Contractor**: Transocean
- **Rig class**: Drillship (ship-shape DP-station-kept floater)
- **Generation**: Gen 7+
- **Rated water depth**: 12,000+ ft (ultra-deepwater)
- **20K-psi HPHT capable**
- **Built**: 2022 (verify at next ingest pass)

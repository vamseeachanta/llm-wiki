# Deepwater Titan

## Scope

Specific named rig entity for **Deepwater Titan** — Transocean's Gen 7+ ultra-deepwater drillship, 20K-psi HPHT capable, sister to Deepwater Atlas. Both are among Transocean's newest and most-capable drillships.

# NORSOK D-010 — Well Integrity in Drilling and Well Operations

## Scope

NORSOK D-010 is the Norwegian Continental Shelf (NCS) standard for well integrity throughout the drilling, production, and abandonment lifecycle. Rev 4 (2021) is the dominant current revision. While NCS-anchored, it's widely referenced globally as the most rigorous practitioner-codified well-integrity framework — many operators apply NORSOK D-010 principles outside Norwegian jurisdiction.

## Two-barrier emphasis

NORSOK D-010 institutionalizes the two-barrier principle as a structural requirement — every flow path requires two verified independent barriers, with explicit definitions of what constitutes a barrier element, how barriers are tested, and how barrier degradation is monitored over the well's life.

## Lifecycle coverage

- **Drilling phase**: primary well-control + BOP-stack integrity + casing integrity
- **Production phase**: tubing + production-packer + Christmas-tree barriers
- **Workover phase**: barriers required during pre-workover suspension
- **Abandonment phase**: P&A barriers — typically a 100-m cross-section cement plug as primary + a 50-m secondary plug above it

## Where it applies

Mandatory for operations on the Norwegian Continental Shelf. Widely referenced internationally by operators seeking the most rigorous well-integrity baseline.

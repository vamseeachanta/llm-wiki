# DNV-OS-E301 Position Mooring

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV offshore standard on position mooring. Covers position mooring system design for floating offshore units — mooring line strength, fatigue limit-state acceptance, line components, and design factors for intact and damaged conditions, including thruster-assisted and pure-passive mooring configurations.

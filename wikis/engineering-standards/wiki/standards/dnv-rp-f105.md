# DNV-RP-F105 Free Spanning Pipelines

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV recommended practice on free spanning pipelines. Provides design methodology for free-spanning pipeline sections subject to vortex-induced vibration (VIV) and direct wave-current loading — span screening, response models for in-line and cross-flow VIV, and fatigue and ultimate-limit-state acceptance.

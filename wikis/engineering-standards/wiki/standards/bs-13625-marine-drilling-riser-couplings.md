# BS EN ISO 13625 — Marine drilling riser couplings

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the BSI-published adoption of ISO 13625 covering marine drilling riser couplings used in offshore drilling operations. Addresses functional, performance, and design verification requirements for riser couplings — including load capacity, fatigue, sealing, and qualification testing — as adopted under UK jurisdiction with technical content mirroring the ISO standard.

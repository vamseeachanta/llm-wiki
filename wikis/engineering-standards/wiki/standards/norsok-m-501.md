# NORSOK M-501 Surface Preparation and Protective Coating

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the NORSOK protective-coating code, covering surface preparation and application of protective coatings on offshore structures, topside equipment, and subsea components on the Norwegian continental shelf — including coating-system selection, durability classes, and acceptance criteria for atmospheric, immersed, and insulated service.

## Why this page exists
This page is the citation resolver target for downstream coating-and-corrosion-aware calc modules under the calc-citation contract. The page contains no clause text, coating-system specification numerics, or thickness-table reproductions from the source. The page serves as an umbrella for both on-disk editions (current 5th Ed 2004 and superseded 4th Ed 1999); calc callers MUST verify against the publisher-current edition before citation use.

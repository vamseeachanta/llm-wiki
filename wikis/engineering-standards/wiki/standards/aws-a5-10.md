# AWS A5.10 — Specification for Bare Aluminum and Aluminum-Alloy Welding Electrodes and Rods

## Scope
Filler-metal specification for bare aluminum and aluminum-alloy welding electrodes and rods. Establishes classification by chemical composition for use in gas metal arc welding, gas tungsten arc welding, plasma arc welding, oxyfuel gas welding, and brazing of aluminum alloys. Covers required chemistry ranges, manufacturing controls, and packaging. Used to specify aluminum filler-metal purchase for code-compliant welding under structural and pressure codes that reference AWS classifications.

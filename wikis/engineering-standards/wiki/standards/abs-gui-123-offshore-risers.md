# ABS GUI-123 Guide for Offshore Risers

## Scope
Guidance document for the design, analysis, fabrication, installation, and in-service inspection of offshore risers under ABS class. GUI-123 covers production, drilling, and export riser systems, with limit-state acceptance criteria and load-combination guidance complementary to API STD 2RD and DNV-OS-F201.

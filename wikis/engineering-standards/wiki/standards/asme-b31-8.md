# ASME B31.8 — Gas Transmission and Distribution Piping Systems (bounded summary)

## Scope

ASME B31.8 prescribes engineering requirements for the design, fabrication, installation, inspection, testing, operation, and maintenance of gas transmission and distribution systems, including gathering pipelines, transmission lines, mains, services, regulator stations, and compressor stations. Class-location concepts that scale design factors with population density are central to its safety architecture.

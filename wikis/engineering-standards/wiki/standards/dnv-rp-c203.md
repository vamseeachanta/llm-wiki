# DNV-RP-C203 Fatigue Design of Offshore Steel Structures

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV recommended practice on fatigue design of offshore steel structures. The recommended practice provides guidance on fatigue analysis methodology and acceptance criteria for welded and machined offshore steel structural details, including S-N curves, design fatigue factors, and stress-concentration handling.

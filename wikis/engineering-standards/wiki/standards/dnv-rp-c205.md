# DNV-RP-C205 Environmental Conditions and Environmental Loads

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV recommended practice on environmental conditions and environmental loads. Covers metocean parameter selection (wind, wave, current, ice, marine growth) and environmental load methodology (Morison-equation regimes, drag and inertia coefficient selection, wave-current interaction) used as load inputs across offshore structural and pipeline analyses.

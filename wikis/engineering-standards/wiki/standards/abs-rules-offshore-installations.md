# ABS Rules for Building and Classing Offshore Installations, Part 1

## Scope
Foundational rule book for the building and classing of fixed and floating offshore installations under ABS class. Part 1 establishes general requirements applicable to offshore installations: classification scope, plan-approval, materials, surveys, and rule administration.

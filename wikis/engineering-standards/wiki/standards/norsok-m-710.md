# NORSOK M-710 Qualification of Non-metallic Sealing Materials and Manufacturers

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the NORSOK qualification code for non-metallic sealing materials used in offshore oil-and-gas service on the Norwegian continental shelf — covering elastomer compound qualification, rapid-gas-decompression testing protocols, manufacturer qualification, and acceptance criteria for sour-service and high-pressure-high-temperature applications.

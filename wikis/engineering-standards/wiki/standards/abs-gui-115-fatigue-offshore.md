# ABS GUI-115 Fatigue Assessment of Offshore Structures

## Scope
Guidance document for fatigue assessment of offshore structures — S-N curve selection, stress-range computation, hot-spot stress methods, and acceptance criteria for fatigue limit-state checks on offshore primary and secondary structural details. The 2014 edition is the latest of three editions on disk (2003, 2010, 2014); the Commentary PDF is a methodology adjunct and is enumerated as a `sources` companion rather than a separate `code_id`.

# ISO 19901-2 — Seismic design procedures and criteria

## Scope
Part 2 of the ISO 19901 series. Specifies seismic design procedures and acceptance criteria for fixed and floating offshore structures: site-specific seismic hazard characterization, two-level (extreme-level and abnormal-level) earthquake checks, and the analysis methods (response spectrum, time-history) appropriate to each. Inputs are consumed by 19902 fixed-steel and 19903 fixed-concrete platform design.

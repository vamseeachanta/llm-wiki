# ISO 19904-1 — Floating offshore structures, Part 1: Ship-shaped, semi-submersible, spar and shallow-draught cylindrical structures

## Scope
Part 1 of the ISO 19904 series. Specifies design requirements for floating offshore structures of ship-shaped, semi-submersible, spar, and shallow-draught cylindrical hull form: hull strength and fatigue, intact and damaged stability, motion analysis, mooring interface, and topsides-to-hull integration. Consumes metocean inputs from 19901-1 and stationkeeping inputs from 19901-7.

# DNV-ST-F101 Submarine Pipeline Systems

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV submarine pipeline systems standard. Covers limit-state design provisions for rigid metallic submarine pipeline systems used to transport hydrocarbons and related media offshore. Renamed from DNV-OS-F101 to DNV-ST-F101 in the 2021 publisher rebrand; the current edition is the 10th Edition (2021).

# DNV-RP-B401 Cathodic Protection Design

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV recommended practice on cathodic protection design. Covers galvanic and impressed-current cathodic protection of submerged offshore structures, pipelines, and components — anode mass and current-output sizing, design current densities by zone and exposure, coating breakdown factors, and design life methodology.

# DNV-RP-F109 On-bottom Stability of Pipelines

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV recommended practice on on-bottom stability of pipelines. Covers absolute, generalized lateral, and dynamic stability methods for submarine pipelines under combined wave and current action — hydrodynamic loading on partially-buried pipes, soil resistance, and stability-criterion selection.

## Why this page exists
This page is a citation resolver for downstream calc modules under the calc-citation contract at `.claude/rules/calc-citation-contract.md`. It contains no clause text, hydrodynamic-coefficient tables, or formula reproductions from the source. RP-F109 closes the pipeline-integrity triplet (with RP-F101 corrosion and RP-F105 free spans) on top of the ST-F101 baseline.

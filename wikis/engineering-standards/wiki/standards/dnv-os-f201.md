# DNV-OS-F201 Dynamic Risers

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV offshore standard on dynamic risers. Covers limit-state design of metallic dynamic risers used for production, drilling, completion, workover, and export between floating units and the seabed — including extreme-response, fatigue, accidental, and serviceability limit states.

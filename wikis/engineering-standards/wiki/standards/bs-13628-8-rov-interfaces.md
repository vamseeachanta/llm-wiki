# BS EN ISO 13628-8 — Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) interfaces

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the BSI-published adoption of ISO 13628-8 covering Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) interfaces on subsea production systems. Addresses interface envelopes, torque tools, tool deployment, force and torque ranges, and operational/maintenance requirements for ROV interaction with subsea hardware as adopted under UK jurisdiction.

# AMPP/NACE TM 0177-96 — Sulfide Stress Cracking Test Methods

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the laboratory test-method standard governing the four standardized procedures used to evaluate metallic-material resistance to sulfide stress cracking and stress-corrosion cracking in wet hydrogen sulfide environments. The test methods (uniaxial tension, bent-beam, C-ring, and double-cantilever-beam configurations) supply the acceptance-criteria evidence cited by the materials-selection standard MR 0175. The on-disk edition is 1996; multiple solution-method revisions have been published since.

This page is the citation resolver target for downstream calc and qualification modules under the calc-citation contract at `.claude/rules/calc-citation-contract.md`.

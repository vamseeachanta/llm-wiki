# ISO 19901-4 — Geotechnical and foundation design considerations

## Scope
Part 4 of the ISO 19901 series. Specifies geotechnical and foundation design requirements for offshore structures: site investigation, soil characterization, axial and lateral pile capacity, shallow-foundation bearing, anchor capacity, and pile-driveability assessment. Provides the soil-load-resistance basis consumed by 19902 (fixed steel), 19904-1 (floating mooring foundations), and 19901-7 (stationkeeping anchor design).

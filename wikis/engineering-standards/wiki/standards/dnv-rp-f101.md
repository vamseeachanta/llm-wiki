# DNV-RP-F101 Corroded Pipelines

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV recommended practice on corroded pipelines. Provides assessment methodology for the remaining pressure-containing capacity of pipelines with metal-loss corrosion defects — single defects, interacting defects, and complex-shape defects — covering both calibrated and partial safety factor approaches.

## Why this page exists
This page is a citation resolver for downstream calc modules under the calc-citation contract at `.claude/rules/calc-citation-contract.md`. It contains no clause text, defect-interaction tables, or formula reproductions from the source. RP-F101 completes the pipeline-integrity triplet alongside RP-F105 (free spans) and RP-F109 (on-bottom stability) on top of the ST-F101 design baseline.

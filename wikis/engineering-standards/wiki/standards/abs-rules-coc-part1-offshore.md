# ABS Rules — Conditions of Classification, Part 1 Offshore

## Scope
Companion classification-gate rule book to the Offshore Installations Rules. Part 1 (Offshore) sets the conditions under which an offshore installation receives, retains, or loses ABS class — survey scope, periodic-survey cycles, damage-survey triggers, and conditions of class-retention.

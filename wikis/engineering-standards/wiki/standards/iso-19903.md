# ISO 19903 — Fixed concrete offshore structures

## Scope
Standard for fixed concrete offshore structures (gravity-base platforms, concrete substructures, and concrete topsides supports). Specifies design loads, reinforced and prestressed concrete member checks, durability requirements, construction tolerances, and in-service assessment. Consumes metocean inputs from 19901-1, seismic inputs from 19901-2, and geotechnical inputs from 19901-4. Sister standard to 19902 (steel) for the concrete material family.

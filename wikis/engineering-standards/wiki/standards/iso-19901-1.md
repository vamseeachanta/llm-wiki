# ISO 19901-1 — Metocean design and operating considerations

## Scope
Part 1 of the ISO 19901 series. Covers metocean design and operating considerations for offshore structures: wind, waves, current, water levels, sea ice, marine growth, and the joint statistics used to derive design conditions. Provides the environmental input that feeds 19902 (fixed steel), 19903 (fixed concrete), 19904-1 (floating), and 19901-7 (stationkeeping).

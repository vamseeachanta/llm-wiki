# DNV-RP-H103 Modelling and Analysis of Marine Operations

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the DNV recommended practice on modelling and analysis of marine operations. Provides methodology for analysing temporary phases of marine operations — lifting through the splash zone, deepwater lowering, transportation by barge or vessel, towing, and installation — including hydrodynamic load models and dynamic-amplification handling.

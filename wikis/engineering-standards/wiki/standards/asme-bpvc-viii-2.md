# ASME BPVC Section VIII Division 2 — Alternative Rules for Pressure Vessels (bounded summary)

## Scope

BPVC Section VIII Division 2 provides alternative construction rules for pressure vessels using more rigorous methods of design, analysis, fabrication, and inspection than Division 1. The alternative design-by-analysis route allows higher allowable stresses in exchange for tighter material, design-margin, examination, and quality-assurance requirements.

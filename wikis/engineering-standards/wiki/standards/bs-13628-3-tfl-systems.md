# BS EN ISO 13628-3 — Through-Flowline (TFL) Systems

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the BSI-published adoption of ISO 13628-3 covering Through-Flowline (TFL) systems for subsea production. Addresses design, components, performance, testing, and operational requirements for TFL servicing of subsea wells under UK jurisdiction. Two BSI on-disk PDFs (Corrigendum 1 only and Corrigenda 1+2) collapse into this single wiki page; both corrigenda iterations apply to the same 2001 base edition.

## Why this page exists
This page is a citation resolver target for downstream calc modules per `.claude/rules/calc-citation-contract.md`. The BS form is the BSI-published adoption of the ISO standard; cite the corresponding ISO `code_id` (`iso-13628-3`) for technical equivalence and this BS page for UK jurisdictional traceability plus BSI corrigendum tracking (the corrigenda are BSI-publication-specific traceability not always carried into the ISO form).

# NORSOK N-001 Structural Design

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the NORSOK structural-design integrity standard, covering general principles for the structural design of offshore steel and concrete structures on the Norwegian continental shelf — limit-state design framework, partial safety factors, and design situations spanning installation, operation, and accidental conditions.

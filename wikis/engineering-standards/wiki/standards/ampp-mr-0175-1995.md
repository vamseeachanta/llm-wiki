# AMPP/NACE MR 0175 (1995) — Sulfide Stress Cracking Resistant Materials, historical edition

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the superseded 1995 single-document edition of the sour-service materials selection standard. The 1995 edition predates the joint NACE/ISO publication and the 2009 multi-part split; it is retained on disk for legacy-traceability purposes only. Its general framework qualifies metallic materials for use in oilfield equipment exposed to wet hydrogen sulfide. Material classes that were later split across Parts 2 and 3 of the 2009 second edition are combined here in a single document.

## Why this page exists
This page exists solely to provide a citation resolver target for legacy calc sites that still cite the 1995 edition without a Part suffix. The calc-citation contract at `.claude/rules/calc-citation-contract.md` requires every standards-derived constant to resolve to a wiki page; a `digitalmodel/` source-file cites bare `MR0175` without an edition tag, and until that caller is migrated, both the 1995 historical edition and the 2009 second edition need resolver targets. The `legacy_code_id` field bridges the 2021 NACE-to-AMPP publisher rebrand. This page is superseded by the 2009 second edition (Parts 1, 2, 3) and by the publisher-current ISO 15156:2020 edition; new calc work must cite the current edition. The page contains no clause text, formulas, or tables.

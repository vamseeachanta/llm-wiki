# ASME B31.4 — Pipeline Transportation Systems for Liquid Hydrocarbons (bounded summary)

## Scope

ASME B31.4 prescribes engineering requirements for pipeline systems transporting liquid hydrocarbons, liquid alcohols, liquid anhydrous ammonia, and other liquids between producers, tank farms, plants, terminals, refineries, and delivery points. It addresses design pressure, materials, fabrication, examination, testing, operation, and maintenance for the in-scope piping.

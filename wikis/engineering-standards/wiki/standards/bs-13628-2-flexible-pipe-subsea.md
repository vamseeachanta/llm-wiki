# BS EN ISO 13628-2 — Flexible pipe systems for subsea (unbonded)

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the BSI-published adoption of ISO 13628-2 covering unbonded flexible pipe systems for subsea and marine applications. Addresses design, material selection, manufacturing, testing, marking, and packaging requirements as adopted under UK jurisdiction with BSI Corrigendum 1 (2001) tracked alongside the BSI publication metadata.

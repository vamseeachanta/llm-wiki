# ISO 19900 — General requirements for offshore structures

## Scope
Umbrella code for the ISO 19900-series. Establishes the general principles, design philosophy and common requirements applicable to offshore structures across the petroleum and natural-gas industries. Each part of the series (19901, 19902, 19903, 19904, 19905) inherits the umbrella's safety-format and limit-state framing.

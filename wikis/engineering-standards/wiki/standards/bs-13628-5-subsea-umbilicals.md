# BS EN ISO 13628-5 — Subsea umbilicals

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the BSI-published adoption of ISO 13628-5 covering subsea umbilicals for offshore production systems. Covers design, materials, manufacturing, testing, and packaging of integrated electric/hydraulic/chemical/optical umbilicals as adopted under UK jurisdiction. The BS form is the BSI-published adoption identical in technical scope to the underlying ISO standard.

# ASME PCC-1 — Guidelines for Pressure Boundary Bolted Flange Joint Assembly (bounded summary)

## Scope

PCC-1 provides guidelines for the assembly of pressure-boundary bolted flange joints. Coverage spans joint preparation, gasket selection guidance, lubrication, bolt-tightening sequence patterns, target-load determination, and qualification of personnel performing the assembly. The guideline is non-mandatory unless invoked by a contract or another code.

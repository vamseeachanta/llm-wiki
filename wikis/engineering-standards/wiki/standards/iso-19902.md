# ISO 19902 — Fixed steel offshore structures

## Scope
Standard for fixed steel offshore structures (jacket-type platforms, towers, and similar pile-founded steel substructures). Specifies design loads, member and joint strength checks, fatigue limit-state acceptance, construction tolerances, in-service assessment, and reassessment of existing platforms. Consumes metocean inputs from 19901-1, seismic inputs from 19901-2, and geotechnical inputs from 19901-4.

# ISO 19901-7 — Stationkeeping systems for floating offshore structures

## Scope
Part 7 of the ISO 19901 series. Specifies design requirements for stationkeeping systems used on floating offshore structures and mobile offshore units: passive mooring (catenary, taut-leg), thruster-assisted mooring, dynamic positioning, and the strength/fatigue limit-state acceptance for line components, anchors, and connectors. Consumes metocean inputs from 19901-1 and geotechnical inputs from 19901-4.

# ABS Rules for Building and Classing Steel Vessels, Part 3

## Scope
Hull construction and equipment rule book for steel vessels classed by ABS. Part 3 covers hull structural arrangement, scantling determination, hull-girder strength, structural details, and hull equipment for classed steel-hulled commercial vessels.

# BS EN ISO 13533 — Drill-through equipment

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the BSI-published adoption of ISO 13533 covering drill-through equipment used in petroleum and natural gas drilling — including blowout preventer (BOP) stack components, drilling spools, adapters, connectors, and associated pressure-containing assemblies. Adopted under UK jurisdiction; the BS form mirrors the ISO standard's technical content for design, manufacturing, testing, marking, and quality requirements.

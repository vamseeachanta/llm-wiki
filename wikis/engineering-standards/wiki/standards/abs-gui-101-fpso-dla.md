# ABS GUI-101 FPSO Dynamic Loading Approach Guide

## Scope
Guidance document for the Dynamic Loading Approach (DLA) applied to FPSO hull strength assessment. GUI-101 sets out the DLA methodology — site-specific loads, response calculations, and acceptance criteria — used as an alternative or complement to the prescriptive ABS Steel Vessels rule-book scantling determination for FPSO units.

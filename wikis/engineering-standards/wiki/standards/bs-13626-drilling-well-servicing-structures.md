# BS EN ISO 13626 — Drilling and well-servicing structures

## Scope
Bounded resolver target for the BSI-published adoption of ISO 13626 covering drilling and well-servicing structures. Addresses design, manufacturing, inspection, and qualification requirements for drilling masts, derricks, substructures, and well-servicing equipment structures used in onshore and offshore petroleum operations. Adopted under UK jurisdiction with technical content mirroring the ISO standard.
